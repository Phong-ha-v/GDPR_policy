Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and shared when you use our app Hungry Hours (App ID: com.oc.grill.haven), in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (such as name, email) if you provide it.
  • Device information (model, OS, unique identifiers).
  • Usage data (actions, in-game behavior, crash reports).
  • Location data (approximate, if allowed).
  • Ad interaction and attribution data via third-party services.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the service.
  • Improve and personalize the user experience.
  • Communicate with you (updates, support).
  • Analyze usage for performance and development.
  • Serve and optimize in-app advertisements.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Your consent.
  • Contract performance.
  • Legal obligations.
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., app analytics, advertising optimization).

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described or comply with legal requirements.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Analytics and crash reporting services (Firebase Analytics, Firebase, MAX Applovin, Appsflyer).
  • Advertising and attribution networks.
  • Third-party service providers working on our behalf.
  • Legal authorities if required.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: support@onechain.game

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.
